1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device when you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, remember your preferences, and provide site owners with information about how the site is used.

This site also uses localStorage — a browser storage mechanism similar to cookies that stores data locally on your device and is never sent to our servers.
