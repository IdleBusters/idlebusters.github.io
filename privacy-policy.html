Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Idle Busters, Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit idlebusters.com. We are committed to keeping things simple and transparent.

1. Who we are

Idle Busters, Inc. is an independent game studio based in the United States, developing PC and mobile games. As the operator of this website, we act as the data controller for any personal information collected through it. You can reach us at:

  • Email: info@idlebusters.com
  • Website: idlebusters.com

2. Information we collect

This website is a simple informational site. We collect minimal data:

Information you provide

  • Email communication: If you contact us via email (e.g. info@idlebusters.com), we receive your email address and the content of your message. We use this solely to respond to you.

Information collected automatically

  • Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser requests a font file, Google may collect your IP address and browser information. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cookie preference: We store your cookie consent choice in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

We do not run our own analytics, use tracking pixels, or collect any personal data beyond what is described above.

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and emails
  • To display the website correctly (fonts)
  • To remember your cookie consent preference

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-party services

The following third-party services are used on this site:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — font delivery. Google Privacy Policy ↗

These third parties may collect certain information from your device according to their own privacy policies. We do not control their data practices.

5. Data retention

Emails you send us are retained only as long as necessary to handle your enquiry, and deleted thereafter. Your cookie preference is stored locally in your browser and is not retained by us.

6. Security measures

We make every reasonable effort to protect any information we handle from unauthorised access, use, or disclosure. We apply modern security practices to safeguard correspondence and data transmitted to us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your data
  • Objection — object to our processing of your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@idlebusters.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. California privacy rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know — request disclosure of the personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to deletion — request that we delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out — we do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • Right to non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights

To submit a California privacy request, contact us at info@idlebusters.com. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

9. Children's privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. International visitors

Our website is hosted and operated in the United States. If you access it from the European Union or other regions with data protection laws, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. Where required, such transfers are carried out using appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission.

11. Data deletion requests

You may request deletion of any personal data we hold about you at any time. To submit a deletion request, email us at info@idlebusters.com with the following information:

We will process your request within 30 days. Please note that we may retain certain information where required by law, or where retention is necessary for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

All correspondence received from you is treated as restricted-access information and handled with strict confidentiality.
